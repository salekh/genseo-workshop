#### **2. Keyword & Entity Usage**
*   **Assessment:** **Good, but room for refinement.**
*   **Proof Keywords:** The briefing lists the correct proof keywords. However, the data highlights **"Apartment"** in related searches (`Familienhotel Mallorca Apartment`). In the briefing, this is currently lumped under "Boutique- & Fincahotels".
    *   *Correction:* "Apartment" and "Aparthotel" are often distinct from "Fincahotels" (rural) and "Boutique" (small/luxury). Many families look specifically for self-catering within a hotel complex.
*   **Entities:** The integration of specific hotel names (Zafiro, VIVA, etc.) and locations (Alcudia, Cala Millor) is well done.

#### **3. Structure & Logic**
*   **Assessment:** **Strong.**
*   **Details:** The funnel from General (Why Mallorca) -> Regional -> Specific (Hotel Type) -> Offers -> Recommendations is logical for a user in the "Consideration Phase."

#### **4. Missing Opportunities & Recommendations**
Despite a strong foundation, the briefing misses a few competitive edges identified in the `topic_clusters` and `competitors` data.

*   **Opportunity 1: Word Count & Depth**
    *   *Observation:* Competitors range from 1,000 to over 6,000 words (Zafiro, Little Travel Society). A target of 1,200–1,500 words may be insufficient to compete for "High Competition" keywords against such comprehensive guides.
    *   *Recommendation:* **Increase target word count to 2,000+ words.** Expand the "Top Recommendations" section to include mini-reviews of the hotels to add bulk and value.

*   **Opportunity 2: Accessibility (Niche Win)**
    *   *Observation:* The Topic Cluster data showed "Barrierefreiheit" has extremely low coverage (1/10 articles).
    *   *Recommendation:* **Add a sub-point regarding "Barrierefreiheit"** (stroller accessibility/wheelchair friendly) under "Ausstattung & Aktivitäten." This is a quick win to outrank competitors for specific long-tail queries.

*   **Opportunity 3: Room Categories**
    *   *Observation:* Topic cluster data shows "Zimmerkategorien" is a distinct topic.
    *   *Recommendation:* Expand on the "Familienzimmer" point. Explain the difference between **"Familienzimmer" (one room, more beds)** vs. **"Apartment" (kitchenette)** vs. **"Connecting Doors"**. This clarifies the "Apartment" keyword issue mentioned in section 2.

*   **Opportunity 4: Visuals/Map**
    *   *Observation:* The briefing is text-heavy.
    *   *Recommendation:* Add a note for the graphics team or author to include a **Map of Mallorca** highlighting the regions mentioned (North vs. East vs. Southwest) to help the user visualize distances to the airport.
